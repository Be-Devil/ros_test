upq

Central image moments

m = upq(im, p, q) is the PQ'th central moment of the image im. That is, the sum of I(x,y).(x-x0)ˆp.(y-y0)ˆq where (x0,y0) is the centroid.

Notes

  • The central moments are invariant to translation.
