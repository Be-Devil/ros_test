hitormiss

Hit or miss transform

H = hitormiss(im, se) is the hit-or-miss transform of the binary image im with the structuring element se. Unlike standard morphological operations S has three possible values: 0, 1 and don't care (represented by NaN).

References

  • Robotics, Vision & Control, Section 12.5.3, P. Corke, Springer 2011.
